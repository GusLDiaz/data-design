Interaction Flow

  1. Ricky views a image on Imgur.com, decides he wants to comment
  2. Imgur prompts him with a text entry field
  3. Ricky writes his comment; hits submit
  4. Imgur writes text to database, returns success token
  5. Ricky sees his comment, and a reply to it, hits reply to that reply
  6. Imgur prompts him with text area
  8. Ricky writes his new comment; hits submit
  9. Imgur writes new "grandchild" reply to database, retains association with original comment
